Purpose

Our biggest focus is to create a carefree environment to play competitive games. We approach this by putting less pressure on you as the gamer so you can play games as games instead of a job and we help put you in a game with positive and fun energetic gamers. Creating this environment and putting you in this mindset will eventually improve your gamesense and your performance will increase.
